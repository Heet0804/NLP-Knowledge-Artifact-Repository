Section D: Real-World Applications

1. Search Engines

Relevant NLP Concepts: Tokenization, TF-IDF, Word Embeddings, Transformer-based Representations.
Why Needed: To understand user queries, process keywords efficiently, and retrieve the most relevant documents.
Expected Benefits: Faster information retrieval, improved search accuracy, and better user experience.

2. Chatbots

Relevant NLP Concepts: Tokenization, Word Embeddings, Contextual Embeddings, Transformer-based Representations.
Why Needed: To understand user input, maintain conversational context, and generate meaningful responses.
Expected Benefits: 24×7 customer support, faster response time, and improved customer satisfaction.

3. Sentiment Analysis

Relevant NLP Concepts: Text Cleaning, TF-IDF, Word Embeddings, Neural Language Models.
Why Needed: To classify opinions expressed in reviews, feedback, or social media posts as positive, negative, or neutral.
Expected Benefits: Better decision making, brand monitoring, and customer feedback analysis.

4. Machine Translation

Relevant NLP Concepts: Tokenization, Word Embeddings, FastText, Transformer-based Representations.
Why Needed: To translate text accurately between different languages while preserving meaning and context.
Expected Benefits: Improved multilingual communication, accessibility, and global collaboration.

5. Text Summarization

Relevant NLP Concepts: Tokenization, TF-IDF, Word Embeddings, Transformer-based Representations.
Why Needed: To condense lengthy documents into concise summaries while preserving the important information.
Expected Benefits: Saves reading time, improves information accessibility, and supports faster decision-making.
